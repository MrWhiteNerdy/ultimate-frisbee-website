About Me

The Man Backstage

His name is Joseph Davis. He's just like you. He works a job. He goes to school for a degree. He even has a family. Yet there's something a little different about him. Every time you mention a disc, his eyes light up a little. Whenever you inquire what game everyone should play, his suggestion is always one of the first heard. He spends his free time honing his skills; countless hours have been spent going through the motions. This is because Joseph is a fanatic; more specifically, he is a Frisbee fanatic.

Why The Site?

This website is devoted to the sport that is called Ultimate Frisbee. To the ones who play it, it's commonly referred to as just Ultimate. For too long, Ultimate has been pushed to the side when it comes time to discuss sports. That time has come to an end. It's time we give the sport the respect it deserves.
